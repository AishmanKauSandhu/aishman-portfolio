About Me

I am a dedicated web developer with a strong foundation in computer information systems, specializing in creating visually captivating and user-friendly web experiences. Proficient in WordPress, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and advanced CSS techniques, I focus on crafting responsive, intuitive websites while implementing best practices in web development and security.

With hands-on experience developing secure login systems, account management features, and cross-browser-compatible web applications, I deliver efficient and secure solutions. From working on freelance projects like MyDigiScope and HopeRoadChildren to excelling in customer service roles, I’ve honed problem-solving, attention to detail, and creativity. Whether improving user engagement or managing databases, my goal is always to ensure seamless functionality and enjoyable web experiences.

Outside of coding, I explore new design trends, stay updated on advancements in cybersecurity, and assist customers in finding practical solutions. Let’s build something amazing together!
